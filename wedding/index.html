Our Love Story

David and Pearly first met in high school chorus class! They had yet to find love (David was adjusting to his newly found baritone sound and Pearly was a mathlete), but became great friends over Allstate performances, drama rehearsals, and pushing David’s obviously dying red Honda Civic through grocery store parking lots to get the ignition started. They stayed connected during college and would visit each other occasionally (Pearly went to see David’s a cappella group perform, and David would come to Muhlenberg to enjoy all of the nightlife that Allentown, Pennsylvania had to offer). They spent more time during a summer together while David was at a computer engineering internship and Pearly was interning in a molecular genetics lab and found out that they really hit it off as a couple as well! They dated long distance through their respective senior years, then decided to skip straight to moving in together while Pearly started her training at Tufts Medical School and David began working for Liberty Mutual as a software developer. They moved into a basement apartment and their relationship flourished amongst takeout pizza from AKs and Breaking Bad episodes, despite Pearly’s long hours, David’s road rage commuting through Boston traffic, and the distinct lack of furniture and pest control in the old apartment building.

By their third year of dating they were moving up in the world – they had found a lovely apartment in Jamaica Plain, could afford date nights other than takeout pizza, and even purchased a bedframe! Pearly started her training in Maine a year later, and David would take the bus up almost every weekend to visit. After a year David decided the bus tickets and Boston apartment lease were adding up, so the couple once again moved in together, this time in Portland, Maine. When time allowed they enjoyed the great outdoors by going camping, hiking, and kayaking. They had been dating for five years when David popped the question (yay!) then sweetened the deal even more by getting her a dog named Sparky one month later. Little did she know that Sparky and David would fall into a deep bromance, and Pearly was the third wheel for a little while until puppy classes, walks, and lots and lots of cheese sticks turned them into a well-functioning trio. For the last year, Pearly has been busy as a resident physician in pediatrics at Maine Medical Center, and David has managed to juggle his job at Liberty Mutual, online classes at Harvard Extension School, and even trained Sparky to twirl, roll over, and give kisses. They are both SO excited to be celebrating their love on June 23rd, 2018, and to share this special moment with all of you!
